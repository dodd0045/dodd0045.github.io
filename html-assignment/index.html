About Me

My name is Ryan Dodd. I am 31 years old. I am creating a new life by starting a career in Interactive Media Design. I was born in Rigaud, Quebec, August 19th 1990. Both my parents moved to Canada From England to find a new successful jobs. Middle child of two sisters. I was raised in Ontario, moving several times throughout my childhood. I didn't stay in one place for too long. I am proud to have spent 4 years in the Canadian Forces serving my country in 2013. I learned to face my fears with courage.

I have spent lots of time working on video production in high school. I love watching movies and seeing the different cinematic style shots and technology used. I enjoy working creatively to come up with new solutions to everyday problems. I hope this career will be a rewarding one using my inherent skills. I believe this path will give me purpose.

There have been some ups and downs in my life, making me think about what matters most. We've all been in situations we wish we could change; although life moves on, we must accept our choices and move forward. My father always said lifes like a book and you have to turn the page to get to a new chapter Today I work very hard to keep myself busy working towards my professional endeavours. You know when you put in the effort, you will get the results. Nothing comes easy.
